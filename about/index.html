    ARTOLDO TV

ARTOLDO (Est. 2015) is a creative collective featuring the film directors and new media artists Sara Ferro and Chris Weil, with a focus on Arthouse Movies, Audiovisual Shows, Avant-garde / Expanded Cinema, Cinebitions, Experimental Films, Feature Documentaries, Immersive Experiences (VR), Moving Images Arts and Video Art Installations. Since 2021 the duo is a proud member of the Etherea Art Gallery by Virginia Monteverde.

In 2022 their works get selected for the AdAC – ARCHIVIO D’ARTE CONTEMPORANEA – Università degli Studi di Genova.
